The binary-star Spica, foremost luminary of the constellation Virgo, stood behind our sun at my moment of birth. visible to the human eye, Spica's remergence in the dawn sky coincided with the harvest season, with world myth ascribing it as the grape-gatherer, the sheaf of wheat, or simply: the bright one.
